How to Play

Choose a mode in the lobby, then use WASD / arrow keys to choose a direction. Your cells keep moving that way.

Press Space to split each large enough player cell, doubling your active cells.

Split cells merge back after about 5 seconds of deep contact with a larger same-team cell.

Hold E to eject mass at about 1% per second.

Small glowing pellets appear over time. Eat them to grow.

Green thorn bushes use their full spiky outline as the real danger zone. Small cells can hide in them, but larger cells can burst into 8 pieces.

Bubble powerups can grant speed, slow you down, or pull nearby objects with a magnet field.

You can absorb another cell only when you are clearly larger than it.

AI rivals will chase prey, run from larger threats, and compete for resources.
